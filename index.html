Day 1 - Mom left for something called Korea

Mom is gone. Dad showed me where on a map. It's very far. He said two weeks. I will count the days.

Gam Gam is here. Kit stayed home with us this morning while Dad drove Mom to the airport. When Dad came back he smelled like coffee and his eyes were wet. I licked his hand. I think that's my job. I don't know what I'm actually supposed to be doing around here.

Then Dad took Kit and Gam Gam to a place called Bubble Planet. I was not invited. When they came home, Dad said he lost his hat in something called a ball pit but found it after 15 minutes. They also had to sign papers about balloon allergies, which Dad thought was weird. I agree. Balloons are suspicious.

After that they went to Arts District and played skee ball and ate lunch. Kit made a 40 point shot. I don't know what that means but everyone seemed impressed. They came home smelling like fries. No fries for me.

Arion got hit in the head with a scooter. He was wearing his helmet. He was fine. Scooters are also suspicious.
